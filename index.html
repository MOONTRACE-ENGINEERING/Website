Our Funding Instrument: ISAFE Notes (Cap + Discount)

We prefer to use the 100x VC's Standard ISAFE (India Simple Agreement for Future Equity) for all early-stage investment rounds, featuring:

  • Valuation Cap: Ensures that your early belief is rewarded ‚Äì you'll convert equity at a fair ceiling, even if the company's valuation grows rapidly. This protects early investors by setting a maximum valuation at which their investment converts into equity.
  • Discount Rate: As an early backer, you'll receive a 10-20% discount on shares compared to new investors in our priced round. This further rewards early-stage risk-takers by allowing them to purchase shares at a lower price than future investors.

This structure simplifies early financing, reduces legal complexity, and aligns incentives ‚Äì allowing us to focus intensely on product development, team building, and achieving market traction, rather than getting bogged down in complex legal negotiations.
